TJ Bell

Engineering Physics Major – Water Testing & Field Operations

TJ Bell is currently pursuing a degree in Engineering Physics at New Mexico State University. His academic interests include experimental physics, data acquisition, and environmentally driven engineering applications.

As part of the 2025 WERC Environmental Design Capstone Project, TJ contributed as the team’s **Water Testing and Field Operations Specialist**. His responsibilities included managing field protocols for collecting and analyzing water samples, conducting on-site pH and turbidity measurements, and validating the integrity of filtration system performance during live testing.

TJ played a vital role in designing and performing heavy metals testing, tracking environmental variables during the experiments, and recording data under changing soil and water conditions. His background in physics helped ensure experimental accuracy and interpret results with scientific rigor.

Passionate about practical applications of science, TJ is committed to applying engineering principles to environmental recovery and sustainability challenges. He believes innovation should empower communities, especially those affected by climate-related disasters.
